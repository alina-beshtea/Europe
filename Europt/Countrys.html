Europe

Europe is a continent located entirely in the Northern Hemisphere and mostly in the Eastern Hemisphere. It is bordered by the Arctic Ocean to the north, the Atlantic Ocean to the west, Asia to the east, and the Mediterranean Sea to the south. It comprises the westernmost part of Eurasia.



There are 44 countries in Europe. A few of them are transcontinental, meaning they are considered to be a part of both Europe and Asia. Some dependencies and territories are also part of Europe such as the Aland Islands, two constituent countries of Denmark, British Overseas Territories, and Svalbard. 28 countries of Europe are members of the European Union (EU). Europe is bdivided into four main regions: Southern, Northern, Eastern, and Western Europe
